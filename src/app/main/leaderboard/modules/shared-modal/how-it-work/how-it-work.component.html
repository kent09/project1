HOW TO EARN?
REWARDS
  1. For every successful signup you referred, you will get 25 SUP
  2. If your referral connects a social media account (atleast one), you will get 75 SUP
TASK POINTS
  1. If your referral completes any task, and is not revoked, you will get 1% from the value of the task reward.
  2. If your 2nd level referral completes any task, you will get half of 1% from the value of the task reward.
  3. If your 3rd level referral completes any task, you will get a quarter of 1% from the value of the task reward.
  4. All Earned Task points will be capped at max 10 SUP reward.
